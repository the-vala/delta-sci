Did someone say Magnetic DNA?!?!

Yes, we did! We have designed a DNA nanoscale device with magnetic properties named "La Caja". This device is capable of interacting and catching different biomolecules (nucleic acids and certain proteins) due to five ssDNA that overhang from the bottom of the device.

The Team

Hi! we are the Delta-Sci team from Monterrey, Mexico, representing the Centro de Investigación y Desarrollo de Educación Bilingüe (CIDEB).


Monterrey is a city located in the northern part of the country, and it is one of the most important and entertaining cities in the whole continent.


The name of the team (Delta-Sci) is composed by the greek letter Delta (Δ) which means change, and Sci, represented by the greek letter Psi (Ψ) for its similar pronunciation to the word Science. Our goal is to make an important contribution to the scientific field and have a great performance in the current Biomod competition.
