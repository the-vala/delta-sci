The Team

Hi! We are the Delta-Sci team from Monterrey, Mexico, representing the Centro de Investigación y Desarrollo de Educación Bilingüe (CIDEB), one of the most important high schools of the Universidad Autónoma de Nuevo León (UANL) because its outstanding participations in national and international competitions in different fields. Most of us are undergraduate students from different carrers, such as Medicine, Actuarial Science, Biotechnology, Chemical Engineer, and many others! All the members have been working really hard for months in order to make this project the best it can be. Learn a little about us!
